Security and Privacy Implications of Remote Proctoring for School Policies and Practices

Who we are

We are an international, interdisciplinary research team investigating the privacy and security aspects of online proctoring tools. Our research centers on the institutional decision-making regarding adoption of online proctoring in higher education institutions. It is one of the three thrust areas of the research project funded by the U.S. National Science Foundation. The other two thrust areas concentrate on students’ and educator’s perception of the security and privacy aspects of online proctoring services. You can find publications related to them here.....
